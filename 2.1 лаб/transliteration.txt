Сtihotvornie pro oktyabr':

October
October's the month
When the smallest breeze
Gives us a shower
Of autumn leaves.
Bonfires and pumpkins,
Leaves sailing down -
October is red
And golden and brown.

Оktyabr'.
Оktyabr' - jeto mesyac,
Кogda dazhe samyj malen'kij veterok
Оsypaet na nas dozhd'
Оsennih list'ev.
Кostry i tykvy,
Пadajushhie list'ya -
Оktyabr' krasnyj,
Зolotoj i korichnevyj.
